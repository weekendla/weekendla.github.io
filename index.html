Welcome!



We are so happy to have you join us for our wedding! We know that it isn't easy to take time out of your schedules to meet us out here so please know that we truly appreciate it. Below you can find some information to help you plan your stay.

The wedding and reception will take place on Saturday, November 8th, 2014. Since the ceremony and reception will be taking place in Central California Coast wine country, we would definitely recommend taking advantage of everything it has to offer by enjoying some wine tastings in the area and making a great weekend out of it. Another option is to stay in cities that are less than a 2-hour drive away, like Santa Barbara, Malibu, and of course, Los Angeles!

The average weather in November in Santa Ynez varies from a high of 72°F to a low of 44°F so please remember to pack some layers. Don’t forget to bring a jacket as the wedding ceremony, dinner, and reception all take place outdoors!

Finally, we are so grateful that you can celebrate with us that no gifts are necessary, your presence is present enough!

Travel & Hotels

As most of you will be traveling to our wedding we recommend you choose one of two ways to enjoy your weekend. You can stay near Santa Ynez and experience Santa Barbara wine country and stay near the venue. Or you could choose to stay near the heart of Los Angeles, enjoy the city and drive to the ceremony.


Flights

There are three surrounding airports that you may fly into that are the most convenient. Usually the best prices will be at LAX (Los Angeles International Airport), however you may also fly into BUR (Bob Hope Airport), or smaller SBA (Santa Barbara Airport).

Check flight prices:


Car Rental

Everyone will require a car to get to the venue from any airport so please don’t forget to make arrangements. It will take about 2.5 hours to drive to the venue from both LAX (Los Angeles) and BUR (Burbank). It will take around 45 minutes to get there from SBA (Santa Barbara).


Hotels

If you don't have time to research some hotels in the area, we have found a couple for you that have very good reviews and are reasonably priced.


  • Petersen Village Inn
    1576 Mission Dr., Solvang, CA 93463

    This inn is very well reviewed and is right in the heart at Solvang. The ammeneties include free parking, free breakfast and free internet. It is about a 10 minute drive to Roblar Winery from here. When we last checked, prices were around $165/night.

  • La Quinta Inn & Suites
    1601 State Street, Santa Barbara, CA 93101

    If you prefer staying in Santa Barbara, this is a reasonably priced and also well reviewed inn. The ammenities also include free breakfast, free internet and free parking. It is a 40 minute drive to Roblar Winery from here. When we last checked, prices were around $180/night.
