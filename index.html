Travel & Hotels

As most of you will be traveling to our wedding we recommend you choose one of two ways to enjoy your weekend. You can stay near Santa Ynez and experience Santa Barbara wine country and stay near the venue. Or you could choose to stay near the heart of Los Angeles, enjoy the city and drive to the ceremony.


Flights

There are three surrounding airports that you may fly into that are the most convenient. Usually the best prices will be at LAX (Los Angeles International Airport), however you may also fly into BUR (Bob Hope Airport), or smaller SBA (Santa Barbara Airport).

Check flight prices:


Car Rental

Everyone will require a car to get to the venue from any airport so please do not forget this. It will take about 2.5 hours from both LAX and BUR. It will take around 45 minutes to get there from SBA.


Hotels

If you don't have time to research some hotels in the area, we have found a couple for you that have very good reviews and are reasonably priced.


  • Petersen Village Inn
    1576 Mission Dr., Solvang, CA 93463

    This inn is very well reviewed and is right in the heart at Solvang. The ammeneties include free parking, free breakfast and free internet. It is about a 10 minute drive to Roblar Winery from here. When we last checked, prices were around $165/night.

  • La Quinta Inn & Suites
    1601 State Street, Santa Barbara, CA 93101

    If you prefer staying in Santa Barbara, this is a reasonably priced and also well reviewed inn. The ammenities also include free breakfast, free internet and free parking. It is a 40 minute drive to Roblar Winery from here. When we last checked, prices were around $180/night.
